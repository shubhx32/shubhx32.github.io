About Me

I am a third year student at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology, Bhopal pursuing my bachelors in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering. I am a passionate researcher interested in tackling problems pertaining to lightweight materials, computational materials science and smart manufacturing.

I have experience conducting research in microstructure evolution and modelling of properties in laser processing of Ti6Al4V, a durable and lightweight Titanium alloy which finds a wide array of applications in the aerospace and defense industries. I have also worked with carbon based materials, studying the synthesis of carbon nanotubes using the Chemical Vapour Deposition (CVD) process and as part of my internship experience at the R&D department at Hindustan Electro Graphites (HEG), Ltd.
